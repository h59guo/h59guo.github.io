Swift: mobile development is interesting! - Fall 2017

I am a well-rounded developer!

While I was doing System Integration Developer at Nulogy, I have a lot of free time after work. One day I was like, "why don't I use them to learn something fun?" Then the first thing that comes to my mind is mobile development. Since I learned some web development when I was in first year, this time I tried to do something different.


iOS is pretty different from C++ or any other language that I learned, because it has such a big library. Howeverm it is not a problem at all! The functions in the library are very useful and powerful, they are so handy that I built a handful of projects just in the first month I learned it.




In particular I built two interesting apps: one is called "tic-tac-toe" and the other is called "Memorable places". check them out on my github.


I bet you played the tic-tac-toe game when you were a kid! It is exactly the same, just on your iphone instead of a piece of paper.

The memorable places app is my favoriate since it is integrated with Apple maps. Users are allowed to choose a place on the Apple map in one ViewController, and that places is then recorded in the TableView in another ViewController. The point of this app is for the user to make a list of the places that they've been to or they want to go, and they can delete any place from the table as they want.
